          Reviving lakes - Collectively

          With a community based approach, we focus on restoring and reviving water bodies - local ponds, lakes and reservoirs. There are wide ranges of technological options for water conservation, use and management. It takes dedicated volunteers and sometimes years of continuous effort to revive a water body. With guidance from conservation experts and local elders, we are able to do this efficiently. We also review periodically to make sure that the working model is sustainable and provides a habitat for biodiversity.
